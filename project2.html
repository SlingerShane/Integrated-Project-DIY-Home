DIY Project 2: Stylish Bookshelf

Build your own stylish bookshelf from scratch with this step-by-step guide.

Materials Needed

  • Wood planks (2x4 or 1x12)
  • Wood screws
  • Sanding block or power sander
  • Wood glue
  • Paint or wood stain
  • Paintbrush
  • Measuring tape
  • Drill

Instructions

  1. Measure and cut the wood to your desired size.
  2. Sand all edges to prevent splinters.
  3. Assemble the sides and bottom using wood screws and glue.
  4. Add shelves at your desired intervals.
  5. Finish with paint or stain to match your decor.
